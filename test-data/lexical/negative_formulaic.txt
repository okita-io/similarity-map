"Then we'll face her together," Rhyannon declared, her voice echoing off the damp stone walls of the hidden passage.

They moved carefully through the tunnel, counting markers scratched into the rock by earlier seekers.

Once seated, he looked out over his assembled nobles with a stern expression. "Today," he began, "we decide the fate of the border holds and the taxes that sustain them."

Courtiers murmured approval while scribes bent over their ledgers, recording every decree.
